Why Epicodus

I recently moved to Portland, Oregon from West Virginia to pursue a career in computer programming. It was a big decision, but something I feel good about. In high school, I loved programming. My computer science course was awesome, and I even developed videogames in my spare time using actionscript 2.0. (Click here to play Mikey RPG! It's not that great.) I went to college a CS major, but quickly realized that my small liberal arts college had little to offer in that department. Instead, I studied English with the dream of becoming a professional author. I guess I'm technically an author because I wrote a book about a video universe and got a short story published in a literay magazine, but I realized that there is a very small market in writing, and decided that I needed a marketable skill! Thus, I decided on programming!
